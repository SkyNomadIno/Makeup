Services


Bridal Makeup:

Bridal makeup is one of the most popular makeup art services. We work with brides to create a customized look that enhances their natural beauty and complements their wedding attire. Bridal makeup may include airbrush makeup, traditional makeup, false eyelashes, and long-lasting products to ensure the bride looks flawless throughout the day.

Special Occasion Makeup:

We provide makeup services for various special occasions, such as proms, parties, anniversaries, and corporate events. The goal is to make the client look and feel their best for the specific event.

Fashion Makeup:

Our makeup artists collaborate with models, designers, and photographers to create avant-garde, runway, or editorial makeup looks that align with the theme of a fashion show or photo shoot.

Makeup Consultations and Lessons:

We offer consultations to help clients choose the right makeup products and colors for their skin type and tone.
